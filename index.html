About Me

I am a Ph.D. Scholar in AI and Computer Vision at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Sejong University, Seoul, Republic of Korea. I work with the Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) Lab, focusing on advanced machine learning, deep learning, remote sensing, anomaly detection, land-cover mapping, earth observation, medical image analysis, object detection, image processing, and semantic segmentation.

My research work includes data analysis, paper writing, project management, research collaboration, student support, and the development of AI-driven computer vision solutions for real-world applications.
